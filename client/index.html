Welcome to Sweetwater Storage
at Bear Lake

Located just minutes from Garden City, Utah, Sweetwater Storage features versatile rental spaces for your RV, Boat, Home Storage, or Commercial use. We offer four different unit sizes ranging from 10' X 12' to 45'x45'. The largest area is a 1600 sq. foot indoor space that includes wooden floors and restrooms. It has previously been successfully used as an office space, dance studio, and preschool. Pricing is competitive and we offer discounts for annual payment. Units are accessible 24 hours a day.
